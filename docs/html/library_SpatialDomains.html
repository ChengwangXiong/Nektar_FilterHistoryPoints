SpatialDomains

SpatialDomain has-a StdRegion

SpatialDomains specifies the geometry of an element. It requires StdRegions for expressing curved faces of the geometry. Examples of the questions that can be asked are:

  • How many vertices?
  • Where are they?
  • Are the edges straight?

A SpatialDomain is made of StdRegions, so in the worse case:

  • A HexGeom has 18 StdQuadExp's (6 faces with 3 dimensions)
  • A SegGeom has 3 StdSegExp's

Since Nektar++ is split in to different libraries, a Mesh Viewer can be made with SpatialDomains, StdRegions, and LibUtilities without compiling the other libraries.
